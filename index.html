What does "Free viewer / Pro viewer / Premium viewer" mean?
F
Free viewer — Someone browsing Biznecto without a paid plan. They can see your company name, country, and trade show tags. Your products and contact are hidden from them.
P
Pro viewer — A paid Pro subscriber browsing the directory. They can see your products and contact info (only if your plan also has products/contact enabled).
⭐
Premium viewer — A Premium subscriber. They can see your full listing including products, contact, AND the contact details of buyer requirements.
Think of it like a two-sided lock: both the viewer's plan and your plan determine what gets shown. If your plan doesn't have products enabled, even a Premium viewer won't see them.
